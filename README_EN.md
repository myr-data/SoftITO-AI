This course was provided in year 2026 by Istanbul Chamber of Commerce (hereby will be referred to as ITO) and SoftITO as "4th Term Artificial Intelligence Programming", and it is the first AI-focused course provided by ITO and SoftITO. Among approximately 1000 people to apply to this programme, I was selected as a part of the best 24 applicants, and I have completed this programme successfully.
